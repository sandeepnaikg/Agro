1. Purpose of the Product
The purpose of this platform is to build a centralized and trusted digital marketplace for eco-friendly agricultural and natural products offered by Avsan Chakra Kernin Eco Solutions Pvt Ltd.
The platform is designed to allow customers to explore, understand, and purchase products in both dried form and powder form through a secure and user-friendly online experience.
A major focus of the platform is to create direct visibility between customers and farmers by connecting customers with a trusted network of  12,000+ farmers, promoting transparency, sustainability, and responsible sourcing.
The primary goal is to make natural and eco-based products easily accessible while supporting farmers, encouraging sustainable agriculture, and building a strong digital presence for the company.
2.  Problem / Pain Point
Customers and businesses often face difficulty finding reliable and eco-friendly agricultural products in one trusted place.
Products are usually spread across multiple sources, and customers may not have complete information regarding quality, availability, and sourcing.
There is also limited direct visibility between farmers and customers, which reduces transparency and trust.
Farmers may also face challenges reaching larger markets and presenting their products directly to customers.
This platform solves these challenges by bringing together all products in one organized digital platform while creating direct farmer connection, clear product information, and a smooth purchasing experience.
3.  Why This Product
The demand for sustainable, natural, and eco-friendly products continues to increase across homes, businesses, and industries.
Customers are looking for trusted products that are easy to access and clearly connected to their source.
This platform helps customers discover products in one place, understand their sourcing, and purchase with confidence.
It also supports farmers by improving market access and increasing visibility of their contribution.
The platform strengthens customer trust, supports sustainable agriculture, and creates long-term growth opportunities for the company.
